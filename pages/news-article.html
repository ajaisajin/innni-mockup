INNNI response to regional nursing workforce plan

INNNI has submitted a comprehensive response to the Department of Health's regional nursing workforce consultation, highlighting the critical contributions of internationally educated nurses across Northern Ireland's Trusts.

Key recommendations

  • Structured onboarding programmes tailored for internationally educated nurses
  • Greater representation in workforce planning forums
  • Investment in mentorship and career progression pathways
  • Recognition of qualifications and experience from diverse healthcare systems

Our full response document is available to members in the Resources section.
