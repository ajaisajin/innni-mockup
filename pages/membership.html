Membership

Join a growing community of internationally educated nurses across Northern Ireland.

Eligibility

Membership is open to internationally educated nurses practising in Northern Ireland, including those in training, newly registered, or established in their careers. Student nurses with an interest in international education pathways may also apply.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to exclusive member resources and publications
  • Event registration and networking opportunities
  • Career support including CV guidance and interview preparation
  • Representation through advocacy and policy engagement
  • Working group participation and leadership pathways
  • Internal announcements and member-only communications

Code of Conduct

All members are expected to uphold professional standards, respect confidentiality, and contribute positively to the INNNI community. Full code of conduct available in our governance documents.
