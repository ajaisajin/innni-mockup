About INNNI

Our vision, mission, and the people leading the network for internationally educated nurses.

Our Vision

A Northern Ireland where internationally educated nurses are fully recognised, supported, and empowered to thrive in their professional careers.

Our Mission

INNNI connects, advocates for, and supports internationally educated nurses through networking, professional development, policy engagement, and wellbeing initiatives.

Aims & Objectives

  • Represent internationally educated nurses at Trust, regional, and policy levels
  • Provide career development and educational signposting
  • Foster peer support and professional community
  • Promote equality, diversity, and inclusion across the nursing workforce

Background

INNNI was established to address the unique challenges faced by nurses who trained outside the UK and now practise in Northern Ireland. We work alongside Trusts, NIPEC, and regional stakeholders to ensure their voices are heard.
